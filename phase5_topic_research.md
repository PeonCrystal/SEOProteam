## Content Optimization Guidelines

### Blog Post Template (High-ROI):
- **H1:** Primary keyword (e.g., "Custom Hockey Jerseys")
- **Meta Description:** 155 chars with keyword + call-to-action
- **Body:** 1,500-2,000 words, answering top 3 related questions
- **Internal Links:** 5-7 links to product pages, related guides
- **CTA:** "Design Your Jersey" or "Get Custom Quote"
- **Images:** 3-5 custom images (jersey photos, design examples)
- **Video:** Embed YouTube tutorial (if relevant)

### Interactive Tools (High-Engagement):
- Jersey Designer (2,600 vol potential)
- Cost Calculator/Price Estimator (320 vol)
- Color Combination Picker (320 vol)
- Size Finder/Fit Guide (450 vol)

### Keyword Density Target:
- Primary keyword: 1.0-1.5%
- Variants: 0.5-1.0%
- Related keywords: 0.5-0.8%
- Avoid keyword stuffing
