How to Use Our Custom Jersey Design Tool

Our online jersey design tool makes it easy to create your team's custom look in minutes. Choose your sport, pick a template, and customize every detail — from colours and logos to player names and numbers. Here's how it works, step by step.

Step 1: Select Your Sport and Jersey Style

Start by choosing your sport — hockey, baseball, or softball. Then select your preferred jersey style from our template library. Each template is fully customizable, so think of it as a starting point rather than a final design.

Available styles include:

  • Hockey — classic, modern, and vintage jersey cuts
  • Baseball — full-button, two-button henley, and pullover options
  • Softball — athletic-cut jerseys in women's and men's fits

Step 2: Choose Your Colours

Select from an unlimited colour palette to match your team's identity. Because we use full dye sublimation, there are no restrictions on the number of colours in your design — and no extra cost for additional colours. Pick your primary, secondary, and accent colours to build a look that's uniquely yours.

Step 3: Add Your Team Logo and Crest

Upload your team logo or crest directly into the design tool. We accept most image formats including PNG, JPG, AI, EPS, and PDF. Don't have a high-resolution logo? Our design team can recreate or enhance your artwork at no extra charge.

Step 4: Customize Names, Numbers, and Details

Add player names, jersey numbers, captain letters, and alternate captain designations. Choose from a variety of fonts and number styles to complete your look. You can also add:

  • Sponsor logos on sleeves, chest, or back
  • Memorial patches or league crests
  • Custom text or taglines

Step 5: Review and Submit Your Design

Once you're happy with your design in the tool, submit it for review. Our design team will refine your mockup into a polished, print-ready proof and send it back for your approval. This step is free, and you can request unlimited revisions until the design is exactly right.

Tips for Getting the Best Results

  • Use high-resolution logos — vector files (AI, EPS, SVG) produce the sharpest results
  • Keep contrast in mind — names and numbers should be easily readable against your jersey colour
  • Think about both sides — consider how the front and back of the jersey work together
  • Don't worry about perfection — our design team cleans up and optimizes every submission

Prefer to Skip the Design Tool?

If you'd rather not use the online tool, that's fine too. You can email us your concept — whether it's a rough sketch, a photo of an old jersey, or just a written description of what you want — and our design team will create a professional mockup from scratch at no cost. Contact us to get started.
