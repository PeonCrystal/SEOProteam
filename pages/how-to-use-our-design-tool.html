Our online hockey jersey builder walks you through the entire design process step by step. No design experience required — just pick your options and we handle the rest.

Drop your team logo or crest directly into the jersey customizer. We accept PNG, JPG, AI, EPS, and PDF formats. Don't have a high-res logo? Our design team can recreate or enhance your artwork at no charge.

Customize player names, jersey numbers, captain letters, and alternate captain designations. Choose from multiple fonts and number styles. You can also add:

Step 5: Submit and Get Your Free Proof

Once your design looks right in the hockey jersey customizer, submit it for review. Our design team refines your mockup into a polished, print-ready proof and sends it back for approval. This step is free, and you get unlimited revisions until the design is exactly right.

Tips for Getting the Best Results from the Jersey Builder

  • Use vector logos — AI, EPS, or SVG files produce the sharpest print results
  • Keep contrast high — names and numbers should be clearly readable against the jersey colour
  • Consider both sides — think about how the front and back designs work together
  • Don't worry about perfection — our designers clean up and optimize every submission before production
