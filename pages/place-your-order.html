Place Your Custom Jersey Order

Ready to order custom jerseys for your team? Here's everything you need to know about placing an order with Pro Team Jerseys — from design to delivery. We make the process simple, transparent, and stress-free.

How to Place Your Order

Option 1: Use Our Online Design Tool

Our jersey design tool lets you build your custom look right in your browser. Select your sport, choose a template, add your colours, logos, and player details, then submit your design for a free professional proof.

Option 2: Send Us Your Concept

Prefer a hands-off approach? Email us your idea — a sketch, a reference image, a description, or even a photo of an old jersey you want to replicate. Our design team will create a professional mockup from scratch at no cost.

What Happens After You Submit

  1. Design proof — within 1–2 business days, we send you a polished digital mockup of your jerseys for review
  2. Revisions — want changes? No problem. We offer unlimited free revisions until the design is exactly how you want it
  3. Approval — once you approve the final proof, your order moves into production
  4. Production — jerseys are produced in approximately 3–4 weeks using full dye sublimation
  5. Shipping — completed jerseys are shipped free to your door (Canada and USA)

What You Need to Provide

To get your order started, have the following ready:

  • Team name and logo — vector files (AI, EPS, SVG) are ideal, but we can work with most formats
  • Colour preferences — specific colours or a general direction you'd like
  • Player roster — a list of names, numbers, and sizes for each jersey
  • Sponsor logos — if applicable, any partner or sponsor artwork to include
  • Design references — optional, but helpful if you have examples of looks you like

Pricing and Payment

Pricing depends on the type of jersey and quantity ordered. There are no setup fees, no art charges, and no colour surcharges — those are all included. Contact us or use our design tool for an instant quote based on your specific order.

  • No hidden fees — the quoted price is the price you pay
  • No minimum order — order one jersey or one hundred
  • Volume discounts available — larger orders receive better per-unit pricing
