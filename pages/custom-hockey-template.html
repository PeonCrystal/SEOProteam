Popular Hockey Jersey Design Styles

Classic Striped Jerseys

The timeless hockey look. Classic striped templates feature horizontal chest and arm stripes in the traditional style you see across professional and amateur leagues. Clean, recognizable, and always sharp on the ice.

Modern and Geometric Designs

Want something more contemporary? Our modern hockey templates incorporate diagonal lines, gradient fades, geometric patterns, and bold colour blocking for a look that stands out in any league.

Vintage and Retro Jerseys

Channel old-school hockey with vintage-style templates featuring simple lettering, minimal striping, and a classic aesthetic that never goes out of style.

Minimalist and Clean Designs

Sometimes less is more. Our minimalist hockey templates use clean lines and solid colour panels for a sleek, professional appearance.

What You Can Customize on Every Template

  • Primary, secondary, and accent colours — match your team's existing palette or create something new
  • Stripe patterns and widths — adjust arm and body striping to your preference
  • Team logo and crest placement — centred chest, shoulder patches, or custom positioning
  • Player names and numbers — choose from multiple fonts and number styles
  • Sponsor logos — add sponsor branding to sleeves, chest, back, or helmet
  • Captain and alternate designations — C and A letter placement
